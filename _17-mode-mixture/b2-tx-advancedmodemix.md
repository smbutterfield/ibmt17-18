## Advanced Mode Mixture

When can we use mode mixture chords?
- when it functions

#### Example: Sample Mode Mixture

Progression: I - bVI - IVM7(#11) - ii%7 - V
- the I, IVM7(#11), and V are from major
- the bVI and ii%7 are from aeolian
